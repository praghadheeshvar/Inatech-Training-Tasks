Mutton Biriyani

Mutton Biriyani is a delicious and hearty dish that is perfect for any occasion. Made with tender mutton, spices, and basmati rice, this dish is sure to please any palate.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb mutton
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for a minute.
  3. Add mutton and fry until it changes color.
  4. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, yogurt, and water.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add rice and mix well.
  7. Cover the pot and cook on low heat until the rice is done.

Serve hot with raita or chutney. Enjoy!
